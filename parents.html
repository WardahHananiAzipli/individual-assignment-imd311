My Beloved Parents

Dear Father and Mother, You taught me so much, and I am so much of it. I will always carry you close to my heart, like a handprint. I would like to express my gratitude for your unwavering understanding of all I have said, everything I haven't said, and everything I never intended to tell you. I'm grateful that you always understand me. You've experienced a great deal of hardship and suffering, but I promise that it won't be in vain. I want to repay you for all of your faith in me. I'll strive to be the greatest version of myself as I grow up.
