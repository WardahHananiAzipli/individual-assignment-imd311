Dear brother and sister, despite our constant fighting regardless of the intensity, our relationship remains unbreakable as siblings. In contrast, little sister may be incredibly annoyying, needy, and spoiled at times. However, at the same time, you are kind, funny, and sweetest thing alive. I am grateful to have you in my life, my lovely sister. Our bond as siblings is sometimes tightly woven, sometimes loosely held but never broke.
